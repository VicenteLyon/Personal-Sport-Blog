About Me and more!

Athlete. Scholar. Sports Business Mind.

I'm Vicente Lyon, a senior at Hobart and William Smith Colleges pursuing dual degrees in Computer Science and Business Management & Entrepreneurship. I'll be continuing at HWS for my master's degree.

As a three-year NCAA Division III football player, I've experienced firsthand what it takes to compete at a high level while maintaining academic excellence. That experience has shaped my passion for the intersection of sports, business, and strategy.
